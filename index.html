About

Yvette Vargas was a former Unveristy of California, Irvine (UCI) graduate with a bachelor's in Film and Media Studies and Comparative Literature. Her past experiences include humanites and social science research, human resources, marketing, film production, jounalism, and teaching. She is currently pursuing her master's at the Unveristy of California, Berkeley (UCB) at the School of Information. Her interests are the intersection of data science, media, and law to elevate oppressed narratives and research subjects.
